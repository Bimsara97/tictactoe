How to Play

Game Rules

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid
  • Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) in empty squares
  • The first player uses X, the second player uses O
  • A player wins by getting three of their symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
  • If all squares are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw

How to Win

To win the game, you need to think strategically:

  • Try to create multiple winning opportunities
  • Block your opponent when they have two in a row
  • Control the center square when possible
  • Watch for diagonal opportunities

Controls

  • Tap or click any empty square to place your symbol
  • Use the restart button to start a new game
  • The game status will show whose turn it is
